Living in a digital age, web archiving is becoming popularized due to websites being abandoned from the early 2000s. With lost digital materials and documents, specialized web archiving materials have surfaced.


For general browsing, Internet Archive 's Wayback Machine is great for viewing sites throughout the years.

To actually archive a website, SingleFile is a browser extension that allows you to save HTML, CSS, and all web materials from a website into a downloadable file.
